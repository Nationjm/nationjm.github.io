Creation of Autonomous Robot and Competition

During the spring semester of 2022, myself and three teammates were tasked with the
ideation and creation of a completely autonomous robot. We had to complete everything from
ideation to creation and everything between. The competition consisted of three challenges.
The first was to remove two ghosts from the competition zone. Second was to collect two
scooby snacks and deliver them to scooby's mouth. The third and final task was to deliver
a 3D printed model of one of the members of Mystery Incorporated.
